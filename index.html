SiamSmile: Welcome to my website

Today's date -

This website is centered around the general theme of Thailand, with a particular focus on Thai language and culture.

Whether it's Thai particles, those little words Thais use all the time but dictionaries never tell you about, or Thailand's famous Loi Gratong festival, this is the website for you.

There's also a section on Isaan dialect, the language spoken by roughly 1/3 of Thailand's population when they're not speaking Thai.

Whatever your interest, I hope you enjoy reading this site at least half as much as I enjoyed making it.

More pages will be added as time and circumstance permit.

Enjoy!
